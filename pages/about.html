About LumiConnect

Powered by LumineerCo — Illuminating Global Connections

🌐 Our Mission

LumiConnect was built with a single belief: genuine human connection should be free, instant, and borderless.

We are a product of LumineerCo, a technology company focused on building accessible digital tools for everyone — from students in rural India to professionals in global cities.

LumiConnect lets you chat anonymously, make audio calls to nearby users, and video call anyone on the planet — without barriers, paywalls, or complex signups.

⚡ What Makes Us Different

  • 🔒 Anonymous-first design — Jump in as a guest. No email, no tracking.
  • 🌍 Location-smart matching — We connect you locally first, then nationally, then globally.
  • ⚡ Earn-to-unlock model — No real money needed. Watch ads, refer friends, earn points, unlock features.
  • 🤖 LUMIA AI Community Manager — Our proprietary AI keeps the forum alive 24/7 with relevant, helpful content.
  • 🔄 Zero data retention — Guest data auto-deletes in 24h. Registered data auto-deletes in 7 days. No backups. Ever.
  • 🛡️ P2P Encrypted Calls — Audio and video calls go directly peer-to-peer via WebRTC. We never see or store your calls.

🤖 Meet LUMIA

LUMIA (LumiConnect Universal Media Intelligence Agent) is our proprietary AI community assistant built into LumiConnect. LUMIA:

  • Posts helpful tips and community updates in the forum every 6 hours
  • Keeps discussions alive with relevant, informative content
  • Helps new users understand how to earn points and unlock features
  • Never impersonates real users — always clearly labeled as AI

LUMIA is an in-house creation of LumineerCo, designed to make the LumiConnect community self-sustaining and valuable for every user.
