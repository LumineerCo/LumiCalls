Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025 | LumineerCo — LumiConnect Platform

⚠️ USE AT YOUR OWN RISK. LumineerCo and LumiConnect are not responsible for any data loss, data leak, unauthorized access, or any harm arising from the use of this platform. By using LumiConnect, you accept full responsibility for your usage and any risks involved.

1. What Data We Collect

Guest / Temporary Accounts

  • Auto-generated username and temporary user ID
  • Approximate geographic location (country and city, via IP)
  • Points balance and activity within the session
  • Chat messages sent during the session

Registered Accounts

  • Username, email address, and hashed password (SHA-256)
  • Country and city (user-provided or IP-detected)
  • Points history, referral code usage
  • Forum posts authored by the user

What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Real names or government-issued identity
  • ❌ Phone numbers (unless provided voluntarily via contact form)
  • ❌ Payment information (all features are points-based)
  • ❌ Audio or video call content (calls are P2P encrypted)
  • ❌ Browsing history outside LumiConnect

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account and session
  • To match you with other users for chat, audio, or video
  • To track and award points based on activity
  • To process referral bonuses
  • To display your forum posts to the community
  • To run geo-based matching (local-first connection priority)

3. Automatic Data Deletion

We operate a strict zero-retention policy:

  • 🕐 Guest accounts: Auto-deleted after 24 hours. No backup, no recovery.
  • 📅 Registered accounts: Auto-deleted after 7 days of inactivity. No backup, no recovery.
  • 💬 Chat messages: Auto-deleted after 48 hours.
  • 🏠 Rooms: Ended rooms purged after 24 hours.
  • 📋 Forum posts: Retained as long as the account exists, deleted with the account.

⚠️ We do not maintain any backups. Once data is deleted, it cannot be recovered by any means, including legal requests.

4. Data Storage

All user data is stored in Google Sheets via Google Apps Script (GAS). By using LumiConnect, you acknowledge that your data is processed and stored using Google's infrastructure and is subject to Google's own terms of service and privacy policy.

We do not use third-party analytics services, advertising trackers, or user profiling tools.

5. Audio & Video Calls

All audio and video calls on LumiConnect use WebRTC peer-to-peer technology. This means:

  • Call data travels directly between users' devices
  • LumineerCo's servers do not intercept, record, or store any call content
  • STUN/TURN servers may be used to facilitate connections; these do not store call content
  • We cannot guarantee call encryption between all network configurations

6. Cookies & Local Storage

LumiConnect uses browser localStorage to store:

  • Your session ID and basic user information (for login persistence)
  • This data is stored only on your device and cleared when you logout

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party cookies.

7. Third-Party Services

  • PeerJS / WebRTC: For P2P audio/video calls
  • Google Apps Script: Backend API and data storage
  • ipapi.co: IP-based geographic location detection
  • Google Fonts: Typography (Oxanium, DM Sans)

Each of these services has their own privacy policies. LumineerCo is not responsible for their data handling practices.

8. Children's Privacy

LumiConnect is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you believe a child has used our service, contact us to request deletion.

9. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have rights to:

  • Access the data we hold about you (limited due to anonymous-first design)
  • Request deletion of your account (data auto-deletes per schedule; contact us for immediate deletion)
  • Opt out of geo-location detection (by not granting browser location permissions)
